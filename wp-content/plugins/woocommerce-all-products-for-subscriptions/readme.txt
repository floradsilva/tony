Attach subscription options to existing WooCommerce products.

== Description ==

Mini-extension for [WooCommerce Subscriptions](http://woocommerce.com/products/woocommerce-subscriptions/) that allows you to: 1) make existing products available on subscription, and 2) let customers add products to their subscriptions. Formerly known as Subscribe All The Things.

Use it to attach subscriptions to Simple & Variable products, [Product Bundles](http://woocommerce.com/products/product-bundles/) and [Composite Products](http://woocommerce.com/products/composite-products/).

**Requires WooCommerce Subscriptions v2.1+**.
